I'm pinning this thread permanently because we get 15+ new posts per week asking about gem generators.

🚫 Gem generators are scams. Every single one. Without exception.
Sites like cocgemshub.com, supercell-rewards.net, gamerewards-hub.com, clashgems-pro.com and similar are phishing pages designed to steal your Supercell login credentials. They cannot and do not add gems to any account. The progress bars and live counters are fake. The "Supercell Partner" badges are fabricated.

Here are the only legitimate ways to get gems:

  • Obstacle removal — clearing trees, mushrooms, and rocks in your village gives 1–6 gems each
  • Achievements — many achievements reward hundreds or thousands of gems upon completion
  • In-game events — Supercell occasionally runs seasonal events with gem prizes
  • Official store — the only way to get large amounts quickly. Buy via Google Play or App Store only

If you entered your credentials on a generator site, change your password at id.supercell.com immediately and enable two-factor authentication.

For those actually looking for legit gem strategies, here's what I do:

💡 Achievement gem farming:
Focus on "Sweet Victory" (win 1,000 multiplayer battles = 1,000 gems), "Champion" trophies achievements, and "War Hero" achievement. Together these can net 3,000–4,000 gems over a few months of active play. Check Builder Base achievements too — people often forget those.

Also always clear obstacles as soon as they spawn. At max village size you get roughly 30–50 gems per year from obstacles alone, but every bit counts.
