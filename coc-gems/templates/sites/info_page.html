How to Get Free Gems in Clash of Clans

Updated March 2026 · 5 min read

Gems are the premium currency in Clash of Clans. While there's no shortcut to getting unlimited free gems, there are several legitimate ways to earn them without spending real money.

⚠️ Scam Warning: Any website or tool claiming to "generate" free gems for Clash of Clans is a scam. Supercell has confirmed that there is no way to add gems to an account externally. These sites exist to steal your Supercell ID credentials. Never enter your password on any third-party website.

Legitimate Ways to Earn Free Gems

  • 🌳

    Clear Obstacles

    Trees, bushes, rocks, and gem boxes spawn in your village. Clearing them earns 0-6 gems each. Gem boxes give 25 gems.

  • 🏆

    Complete Achievements

    In-game achievements reward gems at each tier. "Sweet Victory" (1250 trophies) alone gives 450 gems total.

  • 🎯

    Clan Games

    Monthly Clan Games offer gems as tier rewards. Completing all tiers can earn 50+ gems per season.

  • 💰

    Official Supercell Store Rewards

    The official Supercell Store offers free daily rewards including gems. Visit store.supercell.com to claim yours.

  • ⚔️

    Clan War League Medals

    CWL medals can be exchanged for various rewards. While not directly gems, they provide equivalent value.

  • 🚀

    Events & Special Challenges

    Supercell regularly runs special events that reward gems for completing tasks within a time limit.

Official Supercell Store

The safest way to get gems (both free and paid) is through the official Supercell Store. You can claim daily free rewards and purchase gem packs at standard prices.

How to Spot Gem Generator Scams

Here are common red flags to watch for:

  • Any site promising "unlimited" or "free" gems in exchange for your player tag
  • Sites that ask for your Supercell ID password (Supercell never asks for this on the web)
  • "Human verification" steps that lead to surveys or app downloads
  • Fake progress bars showing "connecting to Supercell servers"
  • Countdown timers and urgency tactics ("offer expires soon")
  • Fake testimonials and user counts
From Supercell's official FAQ: "There is no legitimate way to get free gems outside of in-game methods and our official store. Any website claiming to generate gems is a scam. Never share your Supercell ID credentials with anyone."
